Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about joining and participating in our community garden

How do I join the community garden?

+

Joining is easy! Contact us via phone, email, or the form above to express your interest. We'll schedule a time for you to visit the garden, meet other members, and discuss available plot options. We welcome gardeners of all experience levels.

What are the membership costs?

+

We offer flexible membership options to accommodate different budgets. Individual plots start from KSh 2,000 per season, with family and community rates available. This includes access to tools, water, compost, and educational workshops.

Do I need gardening experience?

+

Not at all! We love teaching new gardeners. Our experienced members and regular workshops will help you learn everything from soil preparation to harvest techniques. Everyone starts somewhere!

What can I grow in my plot?

+

You can grow most vegetables, herbs, and flowers using organic methods. Popular choices include sukuma wiki, spinach, tomatoes, onions, peppers, and various herbs. We provide guidance on what grows best in each season.

Are tools and supplies provided?

+

Yes! We provide basic gardening tools, access to water, compost, and some seeds. Members can also purchase additional supplies through our community orders at discounted rates.

Can I bring my children?

+

Absolutely! We encourage families to garden together. We have special children's activities, educational programs, and family-friendly events throughout the year. Gardening is a wonderful way for kids to connect with nature.
