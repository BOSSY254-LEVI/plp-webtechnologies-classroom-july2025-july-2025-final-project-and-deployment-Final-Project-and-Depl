Stories Behind the Photos

Every image tells a story of growth, community, and connection

First Community Planting Day

March 2021

When we started Greenwood Community Garden, this photo captured the excitement and hope of our very first community planting day. Twenty neighbors came together to plant the seeds of what would become our thriving urban oasis.

Looking at this image now, we can see how those small seedlings have grown into the abundant gardens we enjoy today. It reminds us that great things start with simple acts of coming together.

Our First Harvest Festival

September 2021

This photo captures the joy and abundance of our first official harvest festival. After months of careful tending, we celebrated not just the vegetables and herbs we had grown, but the relationships we had cultivated.

The smiles in this photo tell the story of neighbors who had become friends, children who had learned where food comes from, and a community that had discovered the magic of growing together.

Growing the Next Generation

Ongoing

Some of our most treasured moments happen when we introduce children to the wonder of gardening. This photo shows young hands learning to plant seeds, water seedlings, and understand the cycle of growth.

These children are not just learning about plants ‚Äì they're learning about patience, responsibility, and the connection between humans and nature. They are the future guardians of our green spaces.
